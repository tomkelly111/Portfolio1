About

We are Dublin's premier card room offering Texas No-Limit Hold'Em cash games and tournaments throughout the week. We offer tournaments at all buy-in levels to suit all levels of play from first timers to seasoned veterans. We have a very welcoming athmosphere and drinks are provided on the house while you play the most exciting card game there is!
